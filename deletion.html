Data Deletion Request - Income Planner How to delete your data: In Income Planner – Overtime & Salary Calculator, we prioritize your privacy. All your financial data is stored locally on your device and not on our servers. To delete all your data, you can: Clear App Cache/Data: Go to your Android Settings > Apps > Income Planner > Storage > Clear Data. Uninstall the App: Deleting the app from your device will automatically remove all locally stored salary and overtime records. Request via Email: If you have any questions or specific requests regarding data privacy, please contact us at: [O TEU E-MAIL]. Data Retention: Once you clear the app data or uninstall the app, all information is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.
